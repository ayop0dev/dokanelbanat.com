## 🎨 DESIGN BRIEF

Layout: 5 vertical story frames (1080x1920 each). Each frame represents one era in the history of advertising manipulation. Visual style shifts subtly across frames to reflect the time period — more vintage/sepia early, more digital/neon later. Frame 5 is the brand resolution.

Mood: Documentary and exposing — like a historical investigation. Serious, slightly dark, but building toward empowerment. Each frame lands a specific historical punch.

Colors:
- Frame 1: Warm sepia/cream tones (#f5e6c8) with --color-orange (#ff6b4a) accent — 1920s feel
- Frame 2: Muted mid-century palette with --color-secondary (#ff2d55) bold accent
- Frame 3: High-contrast black and white with --color-primary (#ff009f) highlight lines
- Frame 4: Full digital neon — dark background, --color-primary (#ff009f) and --color-yellow (#ffbe3d) glowing accents
- Frame 5: --color-light (#fff5fb) clean resolution frame with --color-green (#3db5aa) for hopeful CTA

Typography: Rubik Bold for era labels and key statements. MarkaziText for explanatory body copy. Year numbers in Rubik Black extra large as anchor for each frame.

Visual Metaphor: A timeline that evolves — from a vintage newspaper ad aesthetic to a glowing smartphone screen — the mechanism stays the same, only the wrapper changes.

Slide Breakdown:
- Frame 1: Vintage distressed texture background, old newspaper-style graphic of a soap bar with radiating "problem" lines
- Frame 2: Mid-century domestic kitchen silhouette, woman figure with a thought bubble showing judgment
- Frame 3: Abstract body silhouettes, one highlighted as "ideal" surrounded by comparison arrows
- Frame 4: Glowing smartphone screen with heart icons and shopping cart symbols cascading
- Frame 5: Clean brand frame — shield icon with dokanelbanat.com, --color-green accent
