## 🎨 DESIGN BRIEF

Layout: 5 vertical story frames (1080x1920 each). Consistent visual system: each frame has a bold number or short headline in the top third, detailed copy in the middle, and a subtle brand element in the bottom strip. Swipe indicator on right side.

Mood: Honest and empathetic at first, then gradually empowering. The tone shifts from "exposing the problem" (frames 1-3) to "here's how to see clearly" (frames 4-5). Never lecturing — always like a smart friend sharing something important.

Colors:
- Frame 1: --color-primary (#ff009f) bold header text on deep dark background
- Frame 2: Dark background, white text, abstract neural/brain visualization in --color-primary lines
- Frame 3: --color-secondary (#ff2d55) background — the "money connection" revelation frame
- Frame 4: Near-black with --color-yellow (#ffbe3d) accent for the "reality check" items
- Frame 5: --color-light (#fff5fb) clean and hopeful, --color-green (#3db5aa) CTA accent

Typography: Rubik Black for frame headlines and statistics. MarkaziText for body copy. Brand name in Rubik Bold at standard size.

Visual Metaphor: A phone screen showing a "perfect" image, but when tilted or cracked, revealing the production crew, lighting rigs, and editing tools behind it — the illusion exposed.

Slide Breakdown:
- Frame 1: Bold statement on dark background, "291 مراهقة" number as accent
- Frame 2: Abstract brain silhouette with incoming image arrows creating a "comparison loop" visual
- Frame 3: Abstract money flow chart with beauty product silhouettes at the end
- Frame 4: A phone screen shown from the side angle, revealing a "stage set" behind the perfect image — all abstract icons
- Frame 5: Open hands receiving a glowing shield or lightbulb — empowerment iconography
