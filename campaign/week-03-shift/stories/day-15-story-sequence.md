## 🎨 DESIGN BRIEF

Layout: 5 vertical story frames (1080x1920). Contemplative and intimate. Less data, more emotional resonance. Like a quiet conversation with a trusted friend.

Mood: Warm, reflective, healing. The storm of Weeks 1-2 settles into a calm. Early morning energy — soft light, space to breathe.

Colors: Frame 1 soft --color-light (#fff5fb) background with warm accents. Frame 2 warm cream with --color-green (#3db5aa) text. Frame 3 muted warm background. Frame 4 --color-yellow (#ffbe3d) accent with dark text — warmth and insight together. Frame 5 clean white with --color-primary subtle accent.

Typography: MarkaziText Regular for most frames (softer than Rubik). Rubik Bold only for key insight phrases. More line spacing — inviting reading.

Visual Metaphor: Peeling back layers — each frame removes one more layer of conditioning to reveal what was always there underneath.

- Frame 2: Three lines of affirmation — visual breathing room around each

- Frame 4: Single insight line, very minimal design
- Frame 5: Warm invitation with dokanelbanat.com logo
