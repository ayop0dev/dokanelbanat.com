## 🎨 DESIGN BRIEF

Layout: 5 vertical story frames (1080x1920). Frame 1 opens with a poll. Frames 2-4 are data reveal — each one darker and more impactful. Frame 5 is the resolution call to awareness.

Mood: Interactive and revealing — like a friend sharing a disturbing study. Starts casual (poll), escalates to concern, ends with empowerment.

Colors: Frame 1 background --color-light (#fff5fb). Frames 2-3 gradient to --color-secondary (#ff2d55). Frame 4 deep dark background #1a0010 with --color-primary text. Frame 5 --color-green (#3db5aa) for the resolution/hope frame.

Typography: Rubik Bold for all key stats and numbers. MarkaziText for supporting text. Large, readable at mobile story scale.

Visual Metaphor: A descending staircase — each frame is one step deeper into the revelation. Frame 5 shows a door opening.

Slide Breakdown:
- Frame 1: Poll over a phone screen illustration (relatable, warm entry)
- Frame 2: Research citation visualized as a simple data card
- Frame 3: Three arrows (down, up, up) showing the three measured effects
- Frame 4: Loop graphic — algorithm → content → insecurity → purchase → algorithm
- Frame 5: Open door or sunrise visual — the awareness moment
