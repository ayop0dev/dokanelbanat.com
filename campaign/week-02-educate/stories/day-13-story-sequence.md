## 🎨 DESIGN BRIEF

Layout: 5 vertical story frames (1080x1920). Frame 1 is intro setup. Frames 2-4 are the 4 questions, each with a clean visual representation. Frame 5 is the resolution insight.

Mood: Interactive and reflective — like a thoughtful friend asking the right questions. Not judgmental, genuinely helpful. Warm but probing.

Colors: Frame 1 background --color-light (#fff5fb). Frame 2 background --color-secondary (#ff2d55). Frame 3 split green/red visual (#3db5aa and danger red). Frame 4 --color-primary (#ff009f). Frame 5 warm near-white with --color-green text for resolution.

Typography: Rubik Bold for questions. MarkaziText for answer options and body text. Large, readable at story scale.

Visual Metaphor: A magnifying glass examining a purchase decision — zooming in on the moment of decision to reveal what's really driving it.

Slide Breakdown:
- Frame 1: Clean minimal intro card with question count preview
- Frame 2: Instagram Poll sticker embedded over branded background
- Frame 3: Simple red/green traffic light system for the reflective question
- Frame 4: Silhouette buying something alone vs buying for social validation — abstract
- Frame 5: Resolution card with warm design — "الوعي مش بيمنعك تشتري"
