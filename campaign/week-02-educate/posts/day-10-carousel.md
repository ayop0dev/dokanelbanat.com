Layout: 5-slide carousel, square 1080x1080. Slide 1 is impact opener with big stat. Slides 2-3 are mechanism breakdown with step-by-step visual flow. Slide 4 is the consumer awareness moment. Slide 5 is the brand close.

Mood: Investigative but empowering — like a documentary revealing the mechanism, not to scare but to inform. Analytical and sharp.

Colors: Slide 1 background --color-primary (#ff009f). Slide 2 dark charcoal with --color-yellow (#ffbe3d) step highlights. Slide 3 --color-light (#fff5fb) background, dark text, split comparison. Slide 4 dark with --color-green (#3db5aa) checkmarks. Slide 5 --color-primary to --color-secondary gradient.
