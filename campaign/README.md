# dokanelbanat.com — 30-Day Campaign Package

## Campaign Overview

This is the complete 30-day digital content campaign for **dokanelbanat.com** — the first integrated women's ecosystem in the Arab world. The campaign is built around a single, powerful narrative: a global economic machine profits when women feel incomplete. dokanelbanat.com breaks that cycle through awareness, knowledge, and real community.

The campaign spans Instagram, Facebook, and blog.dokanelbanat.com, targeting Arab women interested in conscious lifestyle, entrepreneurship, and small business ownership. All social content is written in Egyptian Arabic (مصري عامي) with natural English technical terms. All blog articles are research-backed and SEO-optimized.

---

## Narrative Arc — Week by Week

### Week 1 (Days 01–07) — Bold & Provocative: Expose the Machine
**Goal:** Stop the scroll. Challenge what the audience thought was normal.
**Tone:** Confrontational, urgent, exposing. Dark colors. Bold type.
**Core message:** A 677-billion-dollar industry profits from your insecurity. That's not an accident. That's design.
**Key stats used:** 677B beauty industry, 70-85% women's consumer spending control.

### Week 2 (Days 08–14) — Educate & Data: How the Mechanism Works
**Goal:** Move from emotional provocation to intellectual understanding.
**Tone:** Analytical, data-driven, investigative journalism energy.
**Core message:** The mechanism is documented. Research shows exactly how it works.
**Key stats used:** 30-minute Instagram study, 291-girl body image study, 58% influencer purchase stat, MENA 46B market, Saudi Arabia 71% negative body image.

### Week 3 (Days 15–21) — Shift: The Alternative Mindset
**Goal:** Bridge from problem exposure to solution. Begin the reset.
**Tone:** Warm, reflective, inviting. Morning light. More white space.
**Core message:** You were never broken. The system told you that. Here's the door out.
**Key pillars referenced:** Self-sufficiency, conscious awareness, minimalism, intentional daily routine.

### Week 4 (Days 22–30) — Empower: You Are Complete. Community. Action.
**Goal:** Arrive. Celebrate. Invite. Convert audience to community and ecosystem.
**Tone:** Celebratory, sisterhood energy, warm and full. CTAs to dokanelbanat.com.
**Core message:** You are complete. The community is here. Take your first step.
**Ecosystem referenced:** Business Academy, Supply Center, Stores, Digital Magazine, Events.

## How to Import calendar.xlsx to Scheduling Tools

**Buffer / Later / Hootsuite:**
1. Open `campaign/calendar.xlsx`
2. Export as CSV from Excel or Google Sheets
3. Use the tool's "Bulk Upload" or "CSV Import" feature
4. Map columns: Date → Schedule Date, Platform → Account, Topic → Caption, File Path → Media

**Meta Business Suite:**
- Use the "Content Planner" feature
- Upload posts individually using the content in each .md file
- Story sequences should be uploaded as multi-frame stories (use each "Frame X" as a separate slide)

**Manual scheduling:**
- Use the publishing schedule table above as your daily checklist
- Each .md file is self-contained: Arabic copy → Design Brief → Image Prompt

## How to Publish Blog Articles to WordPress

Each blog article in `campaign/blog/` contains YAML frontmatter with all metadata:

1. **Create new post** in WordPress dashboard
2. **Copy title** from `title:` field
3. **Set slug** from `slug:` field (Permalink settings)
4. **Set publish date** from `date:` field
5. **Set category** from `category:` field
6. **Add tags** from `tags:` list
7. **Set Yoast/RankMath fields:**
   - SEO Title → `seo_title:`
   - Meta Description → `meta_description:`
   - Focus Keyword → `focus_keyword:`
8. **Copy body** — everything from `## المقدمة` onward
9. **Generate cover image** using the `cover_image_prompt:` with DALL-E, Midjourney, or Firefly
10. Set featured image and publish

**Recommended WordPress plugins:** Yoast SEO or RankMath (for SEO fields), Classic Editor or Gutenberg (both work with this format).
